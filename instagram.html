How do I download my Instagram data?

Instructions to Download Your Instagram Data

  1. Go to your profile and click on "More" (works for the app or browser).
  2. At the bottom, click on "Download your Information" and select "Download or Transfer your Information".
  3. Select "Some of your information".
  4. Under the "Connections" section, only select "Followers and following" and hit "Next".
  5. Select "Download to Device".
  6. Click "Date range" and select "All time".
  7. IMPORTANT: Click "Format" and select "JSON".
  8. Click "Media Quality" and select "Low" (hopefully makes it faster).
  9. Click "Create files" and return when your data is done downloading (this can take about a day sometimes).
